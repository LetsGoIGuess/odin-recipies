Basic Omelette

A very common dish eating for breakfast with onions and eggs cooked with a side of fried potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of olive oil
  • 1/2 pound potatoes sliced thinly
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 large onion thinly sliced
  • 4 large eggs

Steps

  1. Set pan or skillet to medium heat with the oil.
  2. Add the potatoes and season with the salt and pepper, lightly stir the potatoes for about 10-14 minutes until they're golden brown and crisp.
  3. Add onions and continue to stir for 6-8 minutes until they turn brown.
  4. Mix the eggs in the bowl, season the eggs with salt & pepper. Pour the eggs with the potatoes and onions.
  5. Put the heat to low and stir the contents of the pan or skillet for 4-5 minutes and eggs begin to brown at the bottom.
  6. Loosen omelete with spatula by taking a plate or other dish and moving the omelette onto the plate, then slide the uncooked side onto the pan or skillet.
  7. Continue cooking for 4-5 minutes. After time is done, server with side of tomatoes and green onions is preffered.
