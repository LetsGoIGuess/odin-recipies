Airfryer Buttermilk Fried Chicken

Home

Description

This dish takes the basic flavor of plain chicken breast and adds a layer of breading to make it crunchier and tastier, all without it being fried.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pound boneless, skinless chicken thigh
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon seasoned salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • cooking spray

Steps

  • Place chicken thighs into a casserole dish then pour butter milk over the chicken. Let it refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  • Proceed to use the airfryer by preheating it to 380 degrees.
  • Use flour, seasoned salt, and pepper and mix it.
  • Pour the buttermilk chicken into the seasoned mix then coat with panko breading.
  • Prepare area for chicken by spraying the airfryer basket or where the chicken will be placed.
  • Let the chicken cook in the airfyer for around 15 minutes
  • Flip the chicken and let it continue cooking until inside center doesn't look pink or use thermometer to check if the chicken reads 165 degrees.
  • Repeat previous step if there is remaining chicken.
