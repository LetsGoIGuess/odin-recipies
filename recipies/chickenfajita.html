Chicken Fajita

Home

Description

The chicken fajita is a tex-mex dish that blends the sweet taste of chicken breast with a unique flavor of seasoning with a side of vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 8oz Chicken Breast
  • Olive Oil
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 Lime
  • 1 Red Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Gound Cumin
  • Chilli Powder

Steps

  1. Prepping the ingredients
    1. Gather all the ingredients required for making the dish.
    2. Wash the peppers and cut them into thin strips. Place on a plate.
    3. Peel red onion, cut the onion in half, then into strips. Place on the plate.
    4. Cut chicken strips into thin pieces. Place on a plate separate from the vegetables.
  2. Setting the seasoning
    1. Measure 1/2 tablespoon of chili powder and pour into a mixing bowl.
    2. Measure 1/2 tablespoon of ground cumin and pour into mixing bowl.
    3. Measure 1 teaspoon of garlic and pour into mixing bowl.
    4. Measure 1/2 teaspoon of salt and pour into mixing bowl.
    5. Measure 1/4 teaspoon of pepper and pour into mixing bowl.
    6. Mix the contents of the mixing bowl until mixed evenly or to desired mix.
  3. Cooking the Dish
    1. Sprinkle the chicken breast with the season mix evenly.
    2. Place a pan on stove on a medium high heat and add 2 tablespoons of olive oil to pan.
    3. Once the pan is heated up, place the chicken breast onto the pan.
    4. Let the chicken breast cook on the pan for 4 to 5 minutes.
    5. Add sliced bell peppers and onions into the pan and let it cook with the chicken for 2 to 3 minutes.
    6. Squeeze lime juice into the pan as much as you can cover.
    7. After cooking, food can be served with a side of tortilla and any other sides of your choice.
